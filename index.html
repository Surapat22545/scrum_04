FILO
Cat Hotel

FILO aims to provide a loving environment tailored to feline preferences. Each room is designed for exploration, play, and, notably, relaxation. The priority is the happiness and comfort of our guests, minimizing stress during their stay. FILO is committed to a beautiful, clean, safe, and healthy space, treating every cat as our own. Owners can trust us to make FILO a home away from home, ensuring a joyful and stress-free experience for both cats and their owners during vacations or other occasions.
